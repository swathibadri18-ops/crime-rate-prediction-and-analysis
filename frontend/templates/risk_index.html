Real-Time Risk — Dynamic Risk Index
The Dynamic Risk Index calculates a standardized crime risk score on a 0–100 scale based on historical crime intensity. It normalizes yearly crime counts to detect relative escalation or decline in risk levels. This index provides a simplified yet powerful indicator for real-time monitoring.

Current Crime Risk Score

The gauge visualization represents the most recent calculated crime risk score. Higher values indicate elevated risk levels compared to historical benchmarks. This provides an immediate snapshot of current crime intensity within the monitored period.

Risk Score Trend Over Time

The line chart illustrates how risk scores have evolved across years. Increasing patterns indicate rising crime vulnerability, while stable or declining trends suggest improved control. Temporal analysis helps anticipate long-term risk fluctuations.

City-Wise Risk Comparison

This comparison chart ranks cities based on normalized risk scores. It highlights regional disparities in crime vulnerability. Identifying high-risk cities enables targeted deployment and optimized patrol allocation strategies.
