Crime Rate Analysis & Prediction — Criminogenic Factor
Criminogenic Factors focus on understanding the underlying conditions that contribute to criminal behavior. Instead of analyzing crime incidents alone, this module examines social vulnerability, demographic stress, environmental exposure, and situational opportunities. Identifying these root causes helps design long-term crime prevention strategies. This analysis supports policy-level decisions beyond reactive law enforcement.

3.1 Socio-economic Indicators

Analyze demographic vulnerability, policing pressure, and socio-economic risk profiles.

3.2 Environmental Conditions

Study time-based, situational, and spatial crime-enabling environments.
