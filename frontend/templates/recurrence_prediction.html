Recurrence Prediction — Time Until Next Crime
Recurrence Prediction estimates the probability of repeat crime occurrences based on temporal gaps between incidents. It models survival probability and recurrence likelihood across time intervals. This helps anticipate repeat offenses and supports proactive intervention planning.

Survival Curve — Time Until Next Crime

The survival curve represents the probability that no new crime occurs within a specific number of days. A declining curve indicates increasing likelihood of recurrence. This model helps estimate stability periods between crime incidents.

Probability of Repeat Crime

This curve estimates the probability of repeat crime occurrence over time. Increasing probability reflects rising recurrence risk. It supports targeted monitoring during high-risk periods.
