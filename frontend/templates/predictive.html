Predictive Policing leverages historical crime data and advanced machine learning algorithms to anticipate future crime patterns. It analyzes spatial, temporal, and behavioral trends to identify high-risk areas and time windows. This enables law enforcement agencies to deploy resources proactively rather than reactively. Predictive models help reduce crime rates by preventing incidents before they occur. The approach improves patrol efficiency and optimizes manpower allocation. Overall, predictive policing supports data-driven decision-making for safer communities.

2.1 Future Crime Forecast

Predict upcoming crime trends using machine learning models trained on historical data.

Future Crime Forecast predicts upcoming crime trends using historical data and machine learning models. It estimates the likelihood of crimes occurring in specific locations and time periods. This helps authorities prepare preventive measures in advance. Future crime forecasting supports proactive planning and smarter public safety strategies.

2.2 Anomaly Detection

Identify unusual crime patterns and abnormal spikes that deviate from expected behavior.

Anomaly Detection identifies unusual or unexpected crime patterns that deviate from normal trends. It helps detect sudden spikes, rare incidents, or abnormal activity in specific areas or time periods. This analysis enables early alerts for potential threats or emerging crime behaviors. Anomaly detection supports rapid investigation and timely preventive action.
